About IIT Madras

About IIT Madras Established in 1959 by the Government of India, the Indian Institute of Technology Madras is one of the oldest IITs in the country, home to world-class technical education and research as well as industry consultancy and international academic collaboration in sixteen departments and several advanced interdisciplinary research centres. The Instituteoffers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes leading to B.Tech., M.Sc., M.B.A., M.Tech., M.S., and PhD
degrees in a variety of specialisations.

Among its numerous awards and recognitions are the #1 ranking in the National Institutional Ranking Framework's "Overall" category for three consecutive years and the #1 "Engineering Institutions" rank for six consecutive years. It was adjudged India's "Top Innovative Institution" by the Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements in 2019, 2020, 2021 and 2022. IIT Madras was recognised as an Institute of Eminence (IoE) in 2019.
