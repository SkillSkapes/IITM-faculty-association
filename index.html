Faculty Association

Hello, fellow faculty

The purpose of FA is to provide a formal networking platform for faculty members to foster their personal and professional growth. The FA will serve as the voice to represent and advocate for its members, uphold academic freedom, fairness, transparency and equality for all members. FA will increase the awareness of issues specific to the faculty community.

It is my honor to serve you as the President of Faculty Association for the period 2022-2025. I plan to continue to build a strong faculty voice regarding workplace issues and compensation. I hope to use our collective strength and leadership experience here at IITM to support you in making our workplace the kind you look forward to engaging in every day.

Mission

  • Advance and safeguard the rights and welfare of IITM faculty members
  • Represent the personal & professional interests of all faculty
  • Uphold academic freedom, fairness, transparency, and equality for all members
  • Act as a negotiating unit to regulate relations between faculty and the institute
  • Increase awareness to ensure the protection of individual rights

Vision

  • To become the voice of faculty, a source of information, and function as support group for faculty members
  • To advance the cause and rights of faculty members with the institute administration and with the MoE (through proper channels)
  • To interact and seek consensus among FA members of other IITs in matters related to faculty
